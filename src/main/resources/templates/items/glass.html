How to dispose of glass


Empty and rinse containers thoroughly to prevent leftover liquid from contaminating other recyclables, potentially hindering the recycling process.

Reattach lids and caps before recycling to minimize the risk of them being lost during the sorting process; these items can be recycled separately.

Before recycling, remove corks from bottles; however, corks can be composted at home.

When using bottle banks, deposit blue glass alongside green glass.

Avoid placing glass milk bottles in bottle banks or general glass recycling. If your milk is delivered in glass bottles, ensure they are returned to your milkman.
